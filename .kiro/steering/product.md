# StudyFlow AI - Product Overview

StudyFlow AI is an enterprise-grade study management platform that helps students optimize their learning through AI-powered insights and collaborative features.

## Core Value Proposition

Combines advanced AI capabilities (via Heroku Managed Inference and Model Context Protocol) with real-time analytics to help students build better study habits, collaborate effectively, and track their progress.

## Key Features

- **Smart Study Timer**: Pomodoro-style sessions with automatic tracking and coin rewards (1 coin/second)
- **MentorMind AI Assistant**: RAG-powered AI with MCP tool integration for external resource access (costs 100 coins per query)
- **Study Groups**: Real-time collaborative workspaces with messaging and leaderboards
- **Task Management**: Kanban-style todo board with priorities and status tracking
- **Performance Analytics**: Visual dashboards showing study patterns and trends
- **Calendar Integration**: Google Calendar sync for study session planning
- **Spotify Integration**: Music playback during study sessions

## Target Users

Students of all levels who want to:
- Track and improve study habits
- Collaborate with peers in study groups
- Get AI-powered study assistance
- Compete on leaderboards for motivation
- Integrate their study workflow with existing tools (Google Calendar, Spotify)

## Business Model

Freemium with coin-based AI usage:
- Users earn coins through study time
- AI queries cost coins (encourages actual studying)
- Optional BYOK (Bring Your Own Key) for unlimited AI access
